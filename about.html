About

I have never believed that this reality is all there is. There must be something more, just beyond what we can imagine. I can't explain it, so I visualize it.

Benjamin Cahillane is a contemporary artist working from his studio in Switzerland, where his work grows from a fascination with philosophy and architecture. His works are built on genuine silver: light passes through translucent layers of colour, reaches the metal beneath, and returns, so each surface glows from within and shifts as you move before it. Fine lines are scratched back through the colour to expose the silver underneath, and the surface opens into depth: a space you can look into rather than at. They are less pictures than openings onto a reality parallel to our own.
