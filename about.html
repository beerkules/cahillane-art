About

Benjamin Cahillane is a contemporary artist. His paintings are built on genuine precious metal, layered with translucent glazes, then engraved by hand into architectural depth: large-format worlds in silver and shadow.

He began with abstract painting and an early group exhibition in Hamburg. He lives and works in Switzerland.
