About

Each work is its own reality — built on genuine silver, darkened in layers, then engraved by hand into a space that exists only in light.

Benjamin Cahillane is a contemporary artist. His paintings are built on genuine precious metal, layered with translucent pigment glazes and engraved by hand into architectural depth — large-format worlds drawn in silver and shadow.

He began with abstract painting and early exhibitions in Hamburg; his works are held in private collections in Germany, the United Kingdom and Belgium. He lives and works between Germany and Switzerland.
